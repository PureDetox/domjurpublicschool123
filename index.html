Welcome to Domjur Public School

A Legacy of Educational Excellence

Domjur Public School stands as a beacon of educational excellence, committed to nurturing young minds and preparing them for a bright future. Our institution combines Islamic values and ethics with modern teaching methodologies to create a holistic learning environment.

We believe in fostering critical thinking, creativity, and character development alongside academic excellence. Guided by Islamic principles of knowledge, compassion, and integrity, our dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art facilities ensure that every student receives the best possible education.
