Facilities

State-of-the-Art Infrastructure for Excellence

Our Facilities

At Domjur Public School, we believe that a conducive learning environment is essential for academic excellence. Our campus features modern facilities and infrastructure designed to support comprehensive education and holistic development of our students.

Academic Facilities

Smart Classrooms

Fully equipped smart classrooms with interactive whiteboards, projectors, and modern teaching aids. Each classroom is spacious, well-ventilated, and designed for optimal learning.

  • Interactive whiteboards
  • Audio-visual equipment
  • Comfortable seating
  • Natural lighting

Science Laboratories

Well-equipped physics, chemistry, and biology laboratories with modern equipment and safety measures. Students conduct experiments in a safe and supervised environment.

  • Physics Lab
  • Chemistry Lab
  • Biology Lab
  • Safety equipment

Computer Labs

Modern computer laboratories with high-speed internet, latest software, and hardware. Students learn programming, digital skills, and computer applications.

  • Latest computers
  • High-speed internet
  • Programming software
  • Digital resources

Library

Extensive library with thousands of books, journals, and digital resources. Quiet reading areas and study spaces for students to explore and learn.

  • Extensive book collection
  • Digital resources
  • Reading areas
  • Research facilities

Sports & Recreation

Sports Complex

Large sports complex with facilities for various sports including cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, and athletics. Well-maintained fields and courts.

Swimming Pool

Olympic-size swimming pool with modern facilities. Swimming lessons and training programs for students of all skill levels.

Gymnasium

Well-equipped gymnasium with modern fitness equipment. Physical education classes and fitness programs for students.

Indoor Games

Indoor sports facilities including table tennis, badminton, chess, and other recreational activities for students.

Special Facilities

Music & Arts Room

Dedicated spaces for music, dance, and arts. Equipped with musical instruments, art supplies, and performance areas for creative expression.

Cafeteria

Spacious cafeteria serving healthy and nutritious meals. Hygienic food preparation and comfortable dining areas for students and staff.

Medical Room

Well-equipped medical room with first-aid facilities and a qualified nurse. Regular health check-ups and medical support for students.

Transportation

Safe and reliable school bus service covering various routes. Well-maintained buses with trained drivers and safety measures.

Security

24/7 security with CCTV surveillance, access control, and trained security personnel ensuring a safe learning environment.

Wi-Fi Campus

High-speed Wi-Fi connectivity throughout the campus. Students and staff have access to internet resources for learning and research.
