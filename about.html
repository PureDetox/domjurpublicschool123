About Us

Discover Our Story, Mission, and Values

Our Story

Domjur Public School was established in 1999 with a vision to provide quality education that nurtures young minds and prepares them for the challenges of tomorrow. Over the past 25 years, we have grown from a small institution to a leading educational establishment, serving thousands of students and their families.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, academic excellence, and a commitment to holistic development. Rooted in Islamic values and principles, we believe that education is not just about academic achievement, but about shaping well-rounded individuals with strong moral character, ethical values, and a deep sense of responsibility who can contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive, high-quality education that empowers students to achieve their full potential academically, socially, and personally. Guided by Islamic principles of seeking knowledge, compassion, justice, and excellence, we strive to create a nurturing environment where every student feels valued, supported, and inspired to excel.

We are committed to:

  • Fostering academic excellence through innovative teaching methods
  • Developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Instilling Islamic values, ethics, and moral character
  • Building character through compassion, integrity, and respect
  • Promoting physical and mental well-being

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier educational institution that produces confident, capable, and compassionate individuals who are prepared to lead and succeed in an ever-changing world. Our Values

Integrity (Amanah)

We uphold the highest standards of honesty, ethics, and moral conduct in all our actions and decisions, guided by Islamic principles of trustworthiness and responsibility.

Compassion (Rahmah)

We value and honor the dignity, diversity, and unique contributions of every individual in our community, showing kindness and mercy to all.

Knowledge (Ilm)

We believe in the pursuit of knowledge as a sacred duty, striving for excellence in all aspects of education and encouraging lifelong learning.

Justice (Adl)

We promote fairness, equality, and justice in all our interactions, ensuring every student receives equal opportunities and treatment.

Unity (Ummah)

We believe in the power of working together as a community, supporting each other's growth and fostering a sense of belonging.

Service (Khidmah)

We prepare students to be responsible citizens who serve their community and contribute positively to society, following the Islamic tradition of service to others.

Why Choose Us

A Legacy of Excellence

With over 25 years of educational excellence, we have a proven track record of academic achievement and student success. Our graduates have gone on to excel in various fields, from science and technology to arts and humanities.

Holistic Approach

We believe in educating the whole child. Our curriculum balances academic rigor with extracurricular activities, character development, and life skills training.

Modern Facilities

Our campus features state-of-the-art facilities including modern classrooms, science and computer labs, libraries, sports facilities, and more.
