Admission Process

We welcome applications from students seeking quality education. Our admission process is designed to be transparent, fair, and supportive. We accept students from all backgrounds and provide equal opportunities for learning and growth.

Admission Steps

1

Inquiry & Application

Submit an online inquiry form or visit our school office to collect the admission form. Fill out all required details accurately.

2

Document Submission

Submit all required documents including birth certificate, previous school records, photographs, and any other specified documents.

3

Assessment & Interview

Students may be required to take an assessment test and attend an interview with the admission committee (for certain grades).

4

Admission Decision

After review, successful candidates will receive an admission offer letter with details about fees and enrollment procedures.

5

Fee Payment & Enrollment

Complete the fee payment and submit all enrollment documents to secure your child's admission.

Required Documents

Birth Certificate

Original and photocopy

Previous School Records

Report cards and transfer certificate

Photographs

Recent passport-size photos

Medical Records

Health certificate and vaccination records

Parent/Guardian ID

Aadhaar card or other valid ID

Address Proof

Utility bill or rental agreement
