Academics

Comprehensive Educational Programs for Every Student

Our Academic Philosophy

At Domjur Public School, we believe in providing a well-rounded education that goes beyond textbooks. Our academic programs are designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and a love for learning. We follow a curriculum that integrates Islamic values and ethics with modern educational practices, balancing traditional knowledge with contemporary skills, preparing students for success in higher education and beyond while maintaining strong moral and ethical foundations.

Academic Programs

Primary Education

Grades 1-5: Building strong foundations in core subjects with interactive and engaging teaching methods. Focus on literacy, numeracy, and social skills.

  • English Language & Literature
  • Mathematics
  • Science & Environmental Studies
  • Social Studies
  • Islamic Studies & Values
  • Arts & Crafts
  • Physical Education

Middle School

Grades 6-8: Expanding knowledge base with specialized subjects and introduction to advanced concepts. Emphasis on analytical thinking.

  • Advanced Mathematics
  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • History & Geography
  • Islamic Studies & Ethics
  • Computer Science
  • Arabic Language
  • Music & Performing Arts

Secondary Education

Grades 9-12: Preparing for board examinations and higher education with specialized streams and career guidance.

  • Science Stream (PCM/PCB)
  • Commerce Stream
  • Arts & Humanities Stream
  • Computer Science
  • Physical Education
  • Career Counseling

Islamic Studies & Values

Comprehensive Islamic education program that teaches Quranic studies, Islamic history, ethics, and values, integrated with character development and moral education.

  • Quranic Studies & Recitation
  • Islamic History & Civilization
  • Islamic Ethics & Values
  • Arabic Language
  • Character Development
  • Moral Education

Technology Integration

Modern technology integrated across all grade levels to enhance learning experiences and digital literacy.

  • Smart Classrooms
  • Computer Labs
  • Online Learning Platforms
  • Coding & Programming
  • Digital Resources
  • E-Library Access

Teaching Methodology

Interactive Learning

Engaging classroom activities, group discussions, and hands-on experiments to make learning enjoyable and effective.

Project-Based Learning

Students work on real-world projects that develop problem-solving skills and practical knowledge.

Collaborative Learning

Teamwork and peer learning activities that build communication skills and social awareness.

Critical Thinking

Activities designed to develop analytical skills, logical reasoning, and independent thinking.

Creative Expression

Arts, music, drama, and creative writing programs to nurture imagination and self-expression.

Continuous Assessment

Regular evaluations and feedback to track progress and support individual learning needs.
